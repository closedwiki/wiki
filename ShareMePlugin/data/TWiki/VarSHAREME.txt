#VarSHAREME
---+++ SHAREME -- icon bar to share topics on popular social media sites
   * The =%<nop>SHAREME{}%= variable is handled by the ShareMePlugin. Many social media sites are defined in the plugin topic.
   * Syntax: =%<nop>SHAREME%= or =%<nop>SHAREME{"site1, site2"}%=
   * Examples:
      * =%<nop>SHAREME%= shows the default icon set %SHAREME%
      * =%<nop>SHAREME{"Delicious, Facebook, Twitter"}%= shows icons to share the current TWiki topic at Delicious, Facebook and Twitter
   * Category: AdministrationVariables, ExportAndPublishingVariables, LinkingAndNavigationVariables, SkinsAndTemplatesVariables
   * Related: ShareMePlugin
