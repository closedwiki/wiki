#VarDATETIME
---+++ DATETIME -- Display date and time with formatting options and localized dates
   * This variable is handled by the DateTimePlugin.
   * Syntax: =%<nop>DATETIME{[format=]"format"}%=
   * Example: =%<nop>DATETIME{"$wday"}%=, =<nop>DATETIME{"$day $month $year" incdays="-1"}=
