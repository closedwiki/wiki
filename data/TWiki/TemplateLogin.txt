=TWiki::Client::TemplateLogin= is a login manager that you can specify in the security setup section of [[%SCRIPTURL%/configure%SCRIPTSUFFIX%][configure]]. It provides users with a template-based form to enter usernames and passwords, and works with the PasswordManager that you specify to verify those passwords.

See also TWikiUserAuthentication.
