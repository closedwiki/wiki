=TWiki::Client::NoLogin= is a login manager that you can specify in the security setup section of [[%SCRIPTURL%/configure%SCRIPTSUFFIX%][configure]]. It instructions TWiki not to supply login/logout functionality to users. It's the default login manager, so if you want to authenticate users then you should consider TemplateLogin (or maybe ApacheLogin).

See also ClientSession and TWikiUserAuthentication.
