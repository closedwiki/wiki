#VarATTACHFILESIZELIMIT
---+++ ATTACHFILESIZELIMIT -- installation logo image

Maximum size of %TWIKIWEB%.FileAttachments in KB, 0 for no limit.
   * Type: Preference variable defined in [[%TWIKIWEB%.%WIKIPREFSTOPIC%]].  Can be customized for a TWiki site in [[%LOCALSITEPREFS%]], and for each web in their %WEBPREFSTOPIC% topics.
   * Current value: ATTACHFILESIZELIMIT = %ATTACHFILESIZELIMIT%
   * Related: FileAttachments
