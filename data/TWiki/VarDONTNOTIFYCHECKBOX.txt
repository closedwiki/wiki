#VarDONTNOTIFYCHECKBOX
---+++ DONTNOTIFYCHECKBOX -- suppress email notification after editing


Default state of the __Minor Changes, Don't Notify__ (DontNotify) check
box in preview. Check box is initially checked if
=Set DONTNOTIFYCHECKBOX = checked="checked"=, or unchecked if empty.
   * Usage: Evaluated by TWiki skins/templates and not intended to be expanded in any topic.
   * Type: Preference variable - TWikiUserSetting.  Can be customized for a site in [[%LOCALSITEPREFS%]], for each web in their %WEBPREFSTOPIC% topics and by individual [[%MAINWEB%.TWikiUsers][TWikiUsers]] in their home topics.
   * Current value: DONTNOTIFYCHECKBOX = %DONTNOTIFYCHECKBOX%
