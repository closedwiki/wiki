#VarTOPICURL
---+++ TOPICURL -- shortcut to viewing the current topic


This variable always expands to an url pointing to the current base
topic.
   * Type: Preference variable - TWikiRenderingShortcut.
   * Current value: TOPICURL = %TOPICURL%
