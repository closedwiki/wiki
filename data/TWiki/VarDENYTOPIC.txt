---+++ DENYTOPICVIEW, DENYTOPICCHANGE -- access control for topics


These variables define lists of users and groups who may _not_ view or edit
topics, or move them around.
   * Type: Access control - See [[%TWIKIWEB%.TWikiAccessControl]] for a comprehensive description.
