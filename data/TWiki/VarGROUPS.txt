#VarGROUPS
---+++ GROUPS -- a formatted list of groups
   * Expands to a formatted list of user groups in your %WIKITOOLNAME%.
   * Syntax: =%<nop>GROUPS%=
   * The variable is intended to be used in %USERSWEB%.TWikiGroups, to allow a
     group listing for various user mapping managers.
   * Related: [[TWikiVariables#VarREMOTEUSER][REMOTE_USER]], [[TWikiVariables#VarUSERINFO][USERINFO]], [[TWikiVariables#VarUSERNAME][USERNAME]], [[TWikiVariables#VarWIKIUSERNAME][WIKIUSERNAME]], [[TWikiVariables#VarWIKIUSERSTOPIC][WIKIUSERSTOPIC]]
