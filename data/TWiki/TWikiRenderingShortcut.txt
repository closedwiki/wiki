---+++ Rendering Shortcuts -- easy ways to save some typing


Some TWiki preferences are just shortcuts for convenience, to either
save typing or to easily allow line breaks in format strings and the like.
These variables are not intended to be customized, though it is not
forbidden to do so.  Your mileage will vary.
