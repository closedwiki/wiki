#VarSITEMAPLIST
---+++ SITEMAPLIST -- show a web in [[%TWIKIWEB%.SiteMap]]

%STARTINCLUDE%
<!-- intentionally nothing to be included -->
%STOPINCLUDE%

This variable, together with [[TWikiVariables.#VarSITEMAPWHAT][SITEMAPWHAT]] and
[[TWikiVariables.#VarSITEMAPUSETO][SITEMAPUSETO]], control the appearance of the [[%TWIKIWEB%.SiteMap][SiteMap]].

   * For the webs which you want listed in the [[%TWIKIWEB%.SiteMap][SiteMap]], set SITEMAPLIST to =on=, do not set NOSEARCHALL, and supply values for SITEMAPWHAT and SITEMAPUSETO.
   * Type: Preference variable defined in [[%TWIKIWEB%.%WIKIPREFSTOPIC%]].  Can be customized for a TWiki site in [[%LOCALSITEPREFS%]], and for each web in their %WEBPREFSTOPIC% topics.
   * Related: [[TWikiVariables.#VarSITEMAPWHAT][SITEMAPWHAT]], [[TWikiVariables.#VarSITEMAPUSETO][SITEMAPUSETO]], [[%TWIKIWEB%.SiteMap]], %TWIKIWEB%.ManagingWebs
