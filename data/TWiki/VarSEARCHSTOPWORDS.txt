#VarSEARCHSTOPWORDS
---+++ SEARCHSTOPWORDS -- words to exclude from search

Stop words, e.g., common words and characters to exclude from a keyword search
   * Type: Preference variable defined in [[%TWIKIWEB%.%WIKIPREFSTOPIC%]].  Can be customized for a TWiki site in [[%LOCALSITEPREFS%]], and for each web in their %WEBPREFSTOPIC% topics.
   * Current value: SEARCHSTOPWORDS = %SEARCHSTOPWORDS%
