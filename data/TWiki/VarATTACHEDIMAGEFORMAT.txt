#VarATTACHEDIMAGEFORMAT
---+++ ATTACHEDIMAGEFORMAT -- default value for attachment link

Format of images when the __link__ check box is checked.
   * %H% *NOTE:* Keyword ==$name== gets expanded to filename; ==$comment== to comment; ==$size== to ==width="..." height="..."== attribute of ==img== tag; ==\t== to tab and ==\n== to linefeed (3 spaces for bullets).
   * Usage: Evaluated by TWiki skins/templates and not intended to be expanded in any topic.
   * Type: Preference variable defined in [[%TWIKIWEB%.%WIKIPREFSTOPIC%]].  Can be customized for a TWiki site in [[%LOCALSITEPREFS%]], and for each web in their %WEBPREFSTOPIC% topics.
   * Current value: ATTACHEDIMAGEFORMAT = %ATTACHEDIMAGEFORMAT%
