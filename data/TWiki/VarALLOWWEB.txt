---+++ ALLOWWEBVIEW, ALLOWWEBCHANGE -- access control for webs


These variables define lists of users and groups who may view or edit
topics in a web, or move them around.  They must be defined in a web's
!WebPreferences topic and are ignored in other topics.
   * Type: Access control - See [[%TWIKIWEB%.TWikiAccessControl]] for a comprehensive description.
   * Related: [[TWikiVariables.#VarDENYWEBVIEW][DENYWEBVIEW]], [[TWikiVariables.#VarDENYWEBCHANGE][DENYWEBCHANGE]], [[%TWIKIWEB%.TWikiAccessControl]]
