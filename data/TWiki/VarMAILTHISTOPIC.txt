---+++ MAILTHISTOPIC, MAILTHISTOPICTEXT -- mail a link to a TWiki topic


'Mail this topic' mailto: link - can be included in topics, templates
or skins.  It consists of two separate variables making up the link
and the link text.
   * Type: Preference variable defined in [[%TWIKIWEB%.%WIKIPREFSTOPIC%]].  Can be customized for a TWiki site in [[%LOCALSITEPREFS%]], and for each web in their %WEBPREFSTOPIC% topics.
   * Current value: MAILTHISTOPIC = %MAILTHISTOPIC%
   * Current value: MAILTHISTOPICTEXT = %MAILTHISTOPICTEXT%
