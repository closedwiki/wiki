#VarNEWTOPICBGCOLOR
#VarNEWTOPICFONTCOLOR
---+++ NEWTOPICBGCOLOR, NEWTOPICFONTCOLOR -- styles for nonexisting topics


Background and font color for a non existing topic.
   * Type: Preference variable defined in [[%TWIKIWEB%.%WIKIPREFSTOPIC%]].  Can be customized for a TWiki site in [[%LOCALSITEPREFS%]], and for each web in their %WEBPREFSTOPIC% topics.
   * Current value: NEWTOPICBGCOLOR = %NEWTOPICBGCOLOR%
   * Current value: NEWTOPICFONTCOLOR = %NEWTOPICFONTCOLOR%
