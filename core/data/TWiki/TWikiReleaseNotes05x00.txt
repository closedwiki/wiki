---++ Introduction

| __<span style="white-space:nowrap">%X% NOTE:</span>__ | This is Release Candidate 3, not recommended for production use! Below release notes are preliminary for the upcoming TWiki-5.0.0 release. |

TWiki-5.0.0 released on 2010-05-24 is a major release introducing usability enhancements, feature enhancements, and adds extensions to strengthen TWiki as an enterprise collaboration platform.

It is highly recommended to upgrade to TWiki-5.0.0. Users will find this release much more stable and secure in daily use.

__Note:__ !JQueryPlugin and !TopMenuSkin are new in TWiki-5.0.0.
