---+ <nop>TimelinePlugin demonstration topic

Here you should see a demo of the TWiki.TimelinePlugin in action. If not, you should visit =configure= in your installation and enable the plugin.

This timeline will present the WebChanges for this Sandbox.WebHome as a timeline.

%TIMELINE{
"%SCRIPTURL{view}%/Sandbox/TimelinePluginXMLDataInput"
interval="YEAR"
height="450px"
width="450px"
date="%DISPLAYTIME{"$day $month $year"}%"
}%

The timeline gets its input from the TimelinePluginXMLDataInput topic, visitting this topic will show you the events used directly as XML.

To view the topic contents of TimelinePluginXMLDataInput instead (the used SEARCH construct), try editing it directly by using this link: %SCRIPTURL{edit}%/Sandbox/TimelinePluginXMLDataInput.
