---++ UpdateInfoPlugin

The UpdateInfoPlugin handels the new <nop>%ISNEW% tag after a WikiWord.

<nop>WikiWord <nop>%ISNEW% becomes 
	* WikiWord if the Topic is neither new nor updated
	* WikiWord <img src="%PUBURLPATH%/TWiki/UpdateInfoPlugin/new.gif">  if the Topic is younger than five days and is revision 1.1
	* WikiWord <img src="%PUBURLPATH%/TWiki/UpdateInfoPlugin/updated.gif"> if the Topic was changed during the last five days

---+++ Syntax Rules

The <nop>%ISNEW%-Tag must be written after a WikiWord speperates by excatly one Space.

Using full syntax <nop>%ISNEW% takes one ore two options:

	* days: changes the number of days a Topic is marked as new or updated
	* version: specifyes which revision is still considered to be new

---+++ Examples:

	* <nop>MyTopic <nop>%ISNEW%
	* MyTopic %ISNEW%
	* <nop>Web.ToPic <nop>%ISNEW%
	* Web.ToPic %ISNEW%
	* <nop>TopIc <nop>%ISNEW{days="15"}
	* TopIc %ISNEW{days="15"}
	* <nop>TopiC <nop>%ISNEW{days="20" version="1.4"}
	* TopiC %ISNEW{days="20" version="1.4"}
