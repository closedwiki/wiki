---++ TopicClassification in the %WEB% Web

To help organize content within the %WEB% web, the WebForm incorporates a TopicClassification scheme that groups topics in two ways: 
	* *TopicTypes* group topics according to their _function or purpose_ . Some examples might include <nop>WebLog, <nop>MeetingNote, <nop>ResearchNote, or <nop>ReferenceNote. Oftentimes, <nop>TopicType have a characteristic structure which can be defined by a "[[TWiki.TWikiTemplates#Template_Topics][template topic]]".
	* *SubjectAreas* group topics according to _the subject matter_ of the topic. Examples might include <nop>FamilyAndFriends, <nop>SocietyAndCulture, <nop>WebDesign, or <nop>OpenSourceMovement.

*List of TopicTypes used in the %WEB% web:* </br>
%SEARCH{ "[T]opicType.*value\=.*[T]opicType" web="%WEB%" regex="on" nosearch="on" nosummary="on" nototal="on" order="topic" format="	* $topic - $formfield(TopicHeadline)<br>"}% 

*List of SubjectArea in the %WEB% web:* </br>
%SEARCH{ "[T]opicType.*value\=.*[S]ubjectArea" web="%WEB%" regex="on" nosearch="on" nosummary="on" nototal="on" order="topic" format="	* $topic - $formfield(TopicHeadline)<br>"}% 

To organize your topics using this TopicClassification, simply go to CreateNewTopic and follow the instructions there.
