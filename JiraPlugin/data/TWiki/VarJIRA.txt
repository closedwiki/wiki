#VarJIRA
---+++ JIRA -- display JIRA issues found by JQL search
   * This variable is handled by the JiraPlugin. For details, see [[%SYSTEMWEB%.JiraPlugin][JiraPlugin]]
   * Syntax: =%<nop>JIRA{"JIRA URL" jql="..."}%=
   * Example: =%<nop>JIRA{"http://example.com/jira" jql="project = XYZ and status in ('In Progress')"}%=
